

With over twenty-five years of experience, I consider myself a tinkerer, engineer and architect who builds the bridges between enterprise-scale stability and the frontier of autonomous intelligence. My career is defined by an obsession with how systems communicate and how they can be taught to think for themselves.

---

### What I Do

* **Engineering Intelligence**: My recent focus centers on creating systems that act as active participants rather than just passive tools.
* **Reasoning Workflows**: I design workflows where digital agents perform multi-step reasoning to interpret complex, unstructured information.
* **Platform Orchestration**: I treat infrastructure as a programmable foundation that should ideally be invisible to the people using it.
* **Developer Velocity**: Much of my work involves building platforms designed to help other engineers work faster, removing the friction that usually comes with managing complex services across different environments.
* **Legacy Transformation**: I specialize in taking older, rigid systems and rebuilding them into flexible, distributed frameworks that can handle significantly more load while remaining resilient and secure.
* **Automation & Delivery**: My contributions reflect a commitment to automating the "boring" parts of software delivery, ensuring that deployments are as repeatable and hands-off as possible.

---

### My Philosophy

I have navigated everything from co-founded startups to leading modernization efforts at global enterprises. Regardless of the scale, I prioritize architectural clarity and long-term sustainability. I am a firm believer in the power of mentorship and focus on establishing the engineering standards that allow cross-functional teams to innovate at speed.
